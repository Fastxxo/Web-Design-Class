Aubrey Drake Graham

Singer, Songwriter, Rapper.

Aubrey Drake Graham (born 1986) is a Canadian rapper, singer, and actor from Toronto who first gained fame as Jimmy Brooks on Degrassi: The Next Generation before becoming a global music superstar. Known for blending hip-hop, R&B, and pop, he's celebrated for hits like "God's Plan," "Hotline Bling," and mixtapes like So Far Gone, eventually signing with Lil Wayne's Young Money. Drake is a major figure in music, known for chart dominance, emotional lyrics, and entrepreneurial ventures, including OVO Sound
